Gardens by the Bay

Gardens by the bay officially opened on the 29th of June in 2012


The Flower Dome, listed as the largest greenhouse in the world in Guinness Book of Records at 1.2 hectares (12000 square meters)


The iconic Supertree Grove are the 18 tree-like structures which reach heights from 25 to 50 meters. Apart from its pretty looks, it also serves as the critical role of being the garden’s environmental engines, which includes photovolatic cells to harness energy from the sun, the collection of rainwater for irrigation and fountain displays, they also support air intake and exhaust for the conservatories’ cooling systems.
